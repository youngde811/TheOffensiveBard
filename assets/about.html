The Insolent Bard

Version 2.6.4

About This App

The Insolent Bard is your personal Shakespearean insult generator, bringing the wit and venom of Elizabethan England to modern times. Drawing from a pool of over 10,000 authentic insults, the app serves up 1,000 random combinations each session, with the ability to refresh for endless variety.

"Thou art a puking, tickle-brained canker-blossom!"

How It Works

Each insult is crafted using authentic Shakespearean vocabulary, combining three parts:

  • An adjective - describing a quality (e.g., "puking", "artless")
  • Another adjective - adding more color (e.g., "tickle-brained", "elf-skinned")
  • A noun - the final blow (e.g., "canker-blossom", "knave")

With 50 options for each part, the app generates 125,000 unique insults to suit any occasion.

Features

  • Share as Image - Long-press easter eggs to share beautiful parchment-styled images perfect for social media
  • Swipe Gestures - Swipe left on any insult to reveal quick actions: add to favorites (or delete from favorites) and share as image
  • Parchment Card Design (NEW!) - Beautiful card-style list items with depth, shadows, and aged parchment background
  • IM Fell English Font (NEW!) - Classic typeface that balances medieval character with excellent readability
  • Browse Insults - Scroll through 1,000 randomly-selected Shakespearean insults
  • Refresh for More - Tap the refresh icon to load a new random batch from 10,000+ insults
  • Search Insults - Quickly find specific insults with real-time search
  • Multi-Select - Tap multiple insults to select them all at once
  • Send via SMS - Share your favorite insults directly with friends
  • Share - Share insults to any app on your device
  • Save Favorites - Long-press any insult to save it for later
  • Clear All Favorites - Convenient trash icon in favorites header to clear all saved insults at once
  • Settings - Customize haptics, sound effects, volume, and easter egg frequency
  • Sound Effects - Choose between chime or pop sounds when adding favorites (plays only when haptics are off) with adjustable volume
  • Easter Eggs - Discover subtle hidden seasonal surprises - tap for elegant overlay, long-press to share as image
  • Dark Mode - Automatic light and dark theme support
  • Copy to Clipboard - Tap any insult to copy it for use anywhere

Usage Tips

  • Add the home screen widget for a new insult every hour - works completely autonomously once the app has been opened at least once
  • Tap the refresh icon (in the header) to load a fresh batch of 1,000 insults
  • Swipe left on any insult to reveal quick action buttons - heart to favorite (or trash to delete on the Favorites page), and share icon for instant image sharing
  • Tap an insult to select it and copy to clipboard
  • Tap multiple insults to select them all - they'll be copied together, each on its own line
  • Tap a selected insult to deselect it
  • Long-press an insult to add it to your favorites
  • Use the search icon to find specific insults by keyword
  • SMS and Share buttons work with single or multiple selected insults
  • Look for seasonal icons next to random insults:
    • Tap the icon to see the insult in elegant Old English style
    • Long-press the icon to share as a beautiful parchment image
  • Clear all favorites using the trash icon in the favorites page header
  • Visit Settings to toggle haptics, choose sound effects, adjust volume, and change easter egg frequency
  • Turn off haptics in Settings to enable sound effects - choose between a pleasant chime or soft pop sound with adjustable volume
  • Dark mode automatically matches your device's appearance settings

The Technology

This app combines modern mobile development with classic Shakespearean text analysis. The insult database was compiled by analyzing Shakespeare's complete works to extract authentic period vocabulary. The app is built using React Native and Expo, with a Rust-powered generator for creating new insult combinations. Version 2.5.0 introduces a fully autonomous home screen widget that displays a new insult every hour without requiring any app interaction. The widget uses iOS WidgetKit with a 48-hour timeline system, generating randomized batches that automatically refresh. Previous releases introduced intuitive swipe gestures using react-native-gesture-handler, beautiful parchment-colored card designs with depth and shadows, and the elegant IM Fell English font for enhanced readability while maintaining medieval character. The app features shareable parchment-styled images, a convenient "Clear All Favorites" button, and subtle easter egg interactions, all while maintaining intelligent random sampling using the Fisher-Yates algorithm for optimal performance and endless variety.
